HOW ARE OT AND UX SIMILAR

Occupational therapy is a problem solving career which focuses on identifying the barriers and enablers (positive interactions and pain points) which prevent clients from achieving their goals. OTs use client interviews and clinical assessment (usability testing and user research) to identify meaningful user goals and identify the most appropriate evidence based treatment plan (design concept, prototypes and wireframes) to support clients to meet their goals.
